Building and leading high-performing, diverse teams is a cornerstone of my professional journey. I excel at fostering collaboration, nurturing talent, and creating inclusive environments where innovation thrives.

Actions: My team implemented training programmes for installers, carried out strategic analysis to reduce robot faults, and worked collaboratively to optimise operations.

Result: Achieved a 19% reduction in critical failures and enhanced team cohesion across international markets.

Launching the "Robot Guru" Programme

Situation: Fault rates among robots were creating inefficiencies in operations.

Actions: My team developed and delivered a programme to upskill operational staff, improving fault diagnosis and repair capabilities.

Result: Faults reduced by 33%, improving overall robot performance and customer satisfaction.
