Who is Dayna, Really?

Download CV

Hi, I’m Dayna Kivell a tenacious Kiwi who owes her resilience and strengths to being raised by an extraordinary single mother. She taught me that I was enough, instilled in me the importance of finding my passion and joy in life, and showed me the value of hard work and kindness.

I’m a positive, upbeat, and bubbly person who constantly strives to be the best version of myself. Self-awareness is something I value deeply—I’ll always scan the Tube to see if someone needs my seat more than I do, return my empty glasses to the bar at the pub, or ensure my rubbish finds its way to the bin at the cinema. I believe in being the best human I can be, contributing to the world around me and leaving it a little better than I found it.

I’m incredibly self-motivated and try to live life to the fullest by saying “yes” to opportunities whenever they arise. Growth and development are core to my life philosophy, and I actively work to learn and improve myself.

Engineering and problem-solving have been lifelong passions of mine. Ever since I was a child dismantling gadgets to understand how they worked, I’ve been driven by curiosity and the desire to create more efficient solutions. My mind is always ticking, asking, “Is there a better way to do this?”

Outside of work, I find joy in connecting with people, inspiring others through STEM outreach, and exploring life’s adventures—whether that’s saving goals at the weekend as Tooting Bec Reserves goalkeeper, snowboarding down a mountain, learning a new skill like drumming, or simply lending a hand to those in need.

"Engineering is not just about creating technology, it's about creating meaningful solutions for the world."
